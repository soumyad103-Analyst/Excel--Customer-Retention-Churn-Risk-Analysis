# Excel-Customer Retention,Churn & Risk Analysis

# Busines Problem 
The bank is experiencing customer churn but lacks clarity on which customer segments contribute the highest revenue risk and which behavioral indicators are associated with churn. Management requires structured analysis to identify high-value-at-risk customers and design targeted retention strategies.

## Dataset Overview
Personal info, loan details, membership status, churn flag, and risk tier.

## KPIs Designed
Balance at risk, Revenue at risk %, Total customers, Total balance, Churned customers, Churn rate %, Retention rate %, Average balance per customer.

# Analysis Approach
Defined the business questions
Created the necessary columns in raw dataset
Made the pivots tables and charts
Connected pivots to slicers

# Key Insights
Germany churn rate = 37.9%, far above baseline 21.16%. Hence, its Structurally High Risk.
Inactive members show 70% churn rate, which is over 3 times the baseline 21.16%.

# Recommendations
Reactivate Inactive Customers | Churn Customers ↓ Revenue_at_Risk ↓ Retention Rate ↑ | High | Ops Head and Relationship Manager
Address Germany's Structural Churn Imbalance | Churn Rate ↓ | High | Ops Head
